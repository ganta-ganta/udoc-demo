Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about studying abroad or our consultancy services? Explore answers to the most common inquiries from students like you.

1. What services does UMADEVI Overseas Consultancy provide?

We offer guidance on university selection, course application, visa processing, financial documentation, and pre-departure support for students aiming to study abroad.

2. How long does the application process take?

The application timeline varies depending on the country and university. Generally, the process takes 2-6 months from application to visa approval.

3. What documents are required for the student visa process?

Essential documents include a valid passport, admission letter, proof of financial support, academic transcripts, language proficiency scores, and visa application forms.

4. Do you help with scholarships or financial aid?

Yes, we assist students in identifying and applying for scholarships and financial aid programs offered by universities and external organizations.

5. Can I work while studying abroad?

Most countries allow international students to work part-time while studying. We provide guidance on the work regulations for your chosen country.
